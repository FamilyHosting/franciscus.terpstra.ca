Franciscus' repetoire includes pieces from well known classical composers such as Bach and Beethoven, master guitarists such as Francisco Terréga, as well as contemporary composers such as Mason Williams. Many of his pieces were originally written for orchestra or concert ensembles, but have been personally adapted to showcase the guitar's unique versitality.
